Chicken & Chorizo Jambalaya

Description

A healthy Cajun-inspired rice pot recipe that's bursting with spicy Spanish sausage, sweet peppers and tomatoes.

Serve this warming pot to family or friends this weekend. You can also include pork or seafood in addition to chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 chicken breasts, chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 red peppers, thinly sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 225g chorizo, sliced
  • 1tbsp Cajun seasoning
  • 250g long grain rice
  • 400g canned plum tomatoes
  • 350ml chicken stock

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large frying pan with a lid and brown 2 chopped chicken breasts for 5-8 mins until golden.

  2. Remove and set aside the chicken. Tip in the 1 diced onion and cook for 3-4 mins until soft.

  3. Add 2 thinly sliced red peppers, 2 crushed garlic cloves, 225g sliced chorizo and 1 tbsp Cajun seasoning, and cook for 5 mins more.

  4. Stir the chicken back in with 250g long grain rice, add the 400g can of tomatoes and 350ml chicken stock. Cover and simmer for 20-25 mins until the rice is tender.
